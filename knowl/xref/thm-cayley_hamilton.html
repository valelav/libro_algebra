\begin{equation*} B=x^{n-1}B_{n-1}+x^{n-2}B_{n-2}+\dots+B_{0} \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} (x\Idmatrix-A)(x^{n-1}B_{n-1}+x^{n-2}B_{n-2}+\dots+B_{0})=\det(x\Idmatrix-A)\Idmatrix \end{equation*}
ovvero
\begin{equation*} (x\Idmatrix-A)(x^{n-1}B_{n-1}+x^{n-2}B_{n-2}+\dots+B_{0})=x^{n}\Idmatrix+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}\Idmatrix+\dots+a_{0}\Idmatrix\text{.} \end{equation*}
Sviluppiamo ora il membro a sinistra e uguagliando le matrici coefficienti delle rispettive potenze di \(x\) troviamo allora:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} B_{n-1}\amp=\Idmatrix\\ B_{n-2}-AB_{n-1}\amp=a_{n-1}\Idmatrix\\ B_{n-3}-AB_{n-2}\amp=a_{n-2}\Idmatrix\\ \amp\vdotswithin{=}\\ B_{0}-AB_{1}\amp=a_{1}\Idmatrix\\ -AB_{0}\amp=a_{0}\Idmatrix \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
